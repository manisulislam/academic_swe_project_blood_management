About Our Blood Management System

Our Blood Management System is dedicated to ensuring the efficient and transparent management of blood donations and requests. This platform bridges the gap between donors and recipients, offering real-time availability and secure communication.

The system is built using Django for the backend and supports administrative features, donor registration, announcements, and contact support.
